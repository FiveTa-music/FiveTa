قصه گو

دانیال & بیگرز
تاریخ: اردیبهشت 06، 1391

متن موسیقی:

Intro: (Danial Palid & BigRez)

khaste o piyade baz rah miram... bacheha mikham baratun ye dastan begam... ruhe taze'e be dastan midam...

na be un pichidegish balke hezar bar sade tar... bande kafshaee ke lams mikone...zamine zakhmi ke dige taslim shode...

asemune un ruz hamejash abi... zamin mesle hamishe khaki hava garme aftabi... ye nasime velarm zire sayebune derakht...

dust daram velo besham ru in nimkate takht.... dam be dam hey lam bedam man....

Verse 1 (Danial palid):

tu ye dast chaee un yeki bahman barik... ruye pahamam romane talkhiye ke az omresh migzare ye chand sali...

fekr konam toam mikoni azash yadi... be chia delbasti chiaro a dast dadi...

pas dastato mesle man baz kon tu in ghesse parvaz kon...

hamunjuri ke neshaste budam yeho didam yeki az dur umad... yechizi mesle fereshte unvar ba hale'e az nur umad...

ta be un khire shod chesham... mikhastam az khod bi khod besham...

engar khoda ferestade uno az asemun... hess mikonam un vase mane manam vase un...

ba un hamechi baram ye hesse taze bud... besh goftam hichi az donya nemikham to faghat bam bemun...

che shirine negat mano be khab bebar... delneshine sedat naro az yade man...

engar diruz bud ke delam baz shekast... bebin safo sadegham bebin ke ashegham...

coruse Danial (Palid & BigRez):

cheshmat marhame dele pusideye mane bimare...

nabashi ba man vasam dige hamechi tare...

labkhande shirinetam be labe man khande miare... age yeruzi beri misham ye darbedare bichare....

verse 2 (BigRez):

dare mire jelo saat zude zud... khaterehe miad be yadam are ali bud...

pishe ham budim dige ghami nadashtim... mikhastim tuye arezuha raha shim...

picho khame zendegi vasam ye rahe sade bud... vali hala zendegi ye dastane taze khund...

mikham sedaye man bere berese be asemun... ke range abiye un dige shode arezum...

chizi nist ke betune mano arum kone... dari ghadam mizani zire chatr mirize barun doret...

sar paeen arum ghadam zadan toro mibare be un ruzaee ke ye nafar behet migoft vasam bekhun...
