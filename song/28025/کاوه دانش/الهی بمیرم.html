کاوه دانش
تاریخ: شهریور 13، 1391

متن موسیقی:

Elahi bemiram, nabinam shekasti
nabinam ke tanha ye Gushe neshasti
Elahi bemiram, nabinam Ghametro
begu ta bedunam ki Khun kard Deletro
Elahi bemiram, chera Bigharari?
to ke manro dari, na dige Gham nadari
Elahi bemiram, ki Ghalbetro ranjund?
Dele Asheghetro be Asuni larzund
mage to nadashti ye Hamdam mese man?
ke pishet bemune vase par keshidan
mage mordam Eshgham?, man inja neshastam
to harja ke bashi, manam ba to hastam

Elahi bemiram, Cheshat Gerye dare
nemikham bebinam ke har Shab bebare
Elahi bemiram, ki gofte bebandi?
hame Arezume ye Lahze bekhandi
